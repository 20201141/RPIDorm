About Us

RPIdorms is a website created by us: Bomy Rhee, Mei Huang, Shanelle Kan, and Kenneth Dong. We created this website with the idea of helping students choose their dorms, specifically incoming freshmen. We've all experienced the search for the perfect dorm and that experience is not always the smoothest. Through RPI Dorms, our mission is to make that experience as smooth as possible by providing up-to-date photos of each dorm in a medium that is simple and easy.
